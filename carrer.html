Ways to Earn Money as a Student

  • Apply for paid internships on platforms like Internshala, LinkedIn, or company career pages.
  • Freelance in areas like design, coding, writing, and more on Fiverr, Upwork, etc.
  • Start a YouTube channel, blog, or social media page related to your skills or hobbies.
  • Offer tutoring to school students or peers in subjects you’re good at.
